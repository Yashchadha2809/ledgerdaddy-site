Data Processing Addendum (DPA)

Review with legal counsel before publishing. This is a template.

Parties: Customer (Controller) and RUn leadgerdaddy Epicfash Global LLC (Processor)

Last updated:

1. Subject matter & duration

Processing business data to provide LedgerDaddy. Duration: term of your subscription and any legally required retention.

2. Nature & purpose

Parsing documents, creating entries, syncing with integrations, support, security, analytics, and service improvement.

3. Categories of data & subjects

Business contacts, transaction details, documents and images you upload, chat messages and metadata.

4. Processor obligations

  • Process data only on documented instructions from Controller.
  • Maintain appropriate technical and organizational measures.
  • Ensure confidentiality and train personnel.
  • Assist with data subject requests and security notices.
  • Delete or return data at termination, unless law requires retention.

5. Subprocessors

Processor may use subprocessors with written agreements; see Subprocessors list. Controller will be notified of material changes.

6. International transfers

Transfers may rely on SCCs or other valid transfer tools. Details to be finalized with counsel.

7. Audits

Upon reasonable notice, Processor will provide information to demonstrate compliance and allow audits as required by law.

8. Liability

Liability follows the limits set in the main Terms, to the extent permitted by law.
